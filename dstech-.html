Hi, I'm Dasiah Silva

Welcome to DS Tech — your trusted partner for professional IT support and cybersecurity services. I'm Dasiah Silva, a passionate and certified IT specialist dedicated to making technology work for you, not against you.

With over 3 years of hands-on experience in IT support, network administration, and cybersecurity consulting, I've helped many individuals, families, and small businesses take control of their digital environments — safely and confidently.

"Technology should empower you — and protecting it should feel personal, not transactional."

My Approach

I believe great tech support is built on trust, transparency, and genuine care. That means clear explanations, no unnecessary upsells, and real solutions tailored to your specific situation — not cookie-cutter fixes.

Whether you're dealing with a virus, setting up a secure home network, recovering lost data, or looking to fortify your business against cyber threats, I bring the same level of dedication and expertise to every single job.

Why DS Tech?

Choosing the right tech partner isn't just about capability — it's about trust. DS Tech was built on the idea that people deserve IT support that is not only expert and reliable, but also welcoming and easy to work with. We bring a level of care and professionalism to every interaction that sets us apart, because we know that when technology works seamlessly, your whole business runs better.

When you work with DS Tech, you're not just a ticket number — you're a person, and your problem matters. That's a promise I keep with every client, every time.
